An abandoned cart occurs when a shopper adds items but leaves before completing checkout. With industry rates often exceeding 65%, recovery tactics are essential for revenue growth.

Why Carts Get Abandoned

  • Unexpected shipping costs or fees
  • Slow, confusing, or multi-step checkout
  • Site speed issues or simple distractions

Recovery Tactics That Work

  • Send personalized reminder emails with a direct cart link
  • Offer a limited-time incentive (e.g., 10% off or free shipping)
  • Trigger exit-intent popups before visitors leave
  • Streamline checkout; allow guest checkout and show trust badges
